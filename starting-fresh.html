Starting Fresh

Tweet

In Meta.

tags: Random musings

I've decided to move from my MSDN blog to my own WordPress site.  No big deal, since my cobweb of a blog hadn't been updated in years, but you might notice that this site is much younger than most of the posts on it.  I've migrated my old posts and back-dated them to their original publishing date.

My hope is that I'll be publishing to my blog more frequently, and my plan is to open up the blog to non-programming-related topics in an effort to motivate myself to make that happen. Hopefully that strategy works.
