Henry Robinson, the smart Cloudera SDE behind The Paper Trail blog, just posted a great primer on distributed systems.  I highly recommend anyone interested in this field go and take a look - if you've been around long enough, you'll have read most of the papers, but it's a good collection of resources.  I also agree with the person in the comments who recommended Raftbe included, although I would include it in addition to Paxos, and not in lieu of it.
